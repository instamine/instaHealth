ABSTRACT

The evolution of our era has amply simplified users' daily tasks and enhanced the transmission of information flow. Electronic devices hold an essential role in our society and screens become interleaved more than ever to our day-to-day routine. To promote health and break off the chain of immersive screens. The instaHealth mobile application is built to provide solutions and nurture healthy habits. It is conceived to optimize users' screen time and set personal health targets to achieve best outcomes. 

Problem

Technology is evolving at an unprecedented pace, and people are embracing these rates of changes incrementally by integrating them into their daily routines without evaluating the impacts on their physical and mental health.
The immersive flow of technology brings an intensive use of screens due to the ease of information access. There is an evidence of a direct correlation between the longer screen time use and the odds of developing a dependency. Screen dependency has negative impacts which affects daily essentials such as sleeping, eating and exercising on time, it also compromises personal time for meditating, walking and socializing etc. Many applications have tried to address these issues however, the adequacy and the approaches provided did not meet the end-users expected goal.
The lack of a holistic solution that limits screen time by creatively motivating users and reminding them of their roadmap, goals and insights has gotten consumers to avoid using health applications regularly, as it defeats their ultimate benefit of breaking their bad habits and get them going into their journey.

Solution

Our solution is a monolithic application that combines extensive health features in one platform, depending on users goal, it also provides a keen sense of interactivity and a smooth user experience. InstaHealth encapsulates AI trackers and designs a whole new set of features to perfectly align with all ranges of users.
To spark user’s interest instaHealth gives health related recommendations, suggestions and interpolations for future predictions and general insights of progress, roadmap and goal. It also provides specific notification personalized advice. InstaHealth gives back the control to the user to adopt optimal healthy habits, reduce screen time and apply heuristic solutions to best accommodate users of using blue filters or/and screen light adoption to prevent eye stains and achieve best mental and physical health outcomes.

Final important point, people are considered moderate to high risk to experience future health issues based on the survey, it shows that 66.7% of the users do not perform the minimum time required to exercise daily/weekly.

Colors

The health of our users is our first priority, the team picked colors accordingly by taking into account that fact that 6 % of the population have deuteranomaly and deuteranopia affects in other terms they are colorblind [7]. instaHealth team picked wisely the colors to meet everyone's best visual experience.


Below on the color palette, it shows the true color how it is suppose to be perceived and its impact on colorblind poeple (Prot. and Deut.). At first the team highlighted the green color and it was our first pick ro represent health and freshness therefore, based on this research instaHealth team discarded the green color as it defeats the color vibration frequency of our application in the eyes of the colorblind users.  Thus, the team selected the best hue ranges between dark purple all away to light Blue to meet all users best experience.

Conclusion

It was a wonderful experience to try to create the best design possible which intrigues the users best interest and experience while being appealing and pleasing to the eyes. The team went through many challenges thus, we have indeed learned a lot. The most important challenge was when the team tried to please everybody by considering minorities for instance colorblind and old users. This idea has brought a whole new wind of creativity as it was totally unexpected. It was challenging combing this and trying to stick to the main goal of restricting screen times. instaHealth tried to make the application intuitive by applying many icons instead of text to simplify it for old people as well for those who struggle with eyestrains. The icons were bigger than previously anticipated and the colors were restricted between purple and blue. The team thinks that the project is a success considering the amount of time spent. Two things, instaHealth will consider in the future is to never try to please everybody as the devil is in the details and it might get very complex than what it supposed to be. The second point is to enhance the coordination and communicate constantly   our design and thoughts before implementing it. Overall, it is a wonderful experience of making the first design prototype ever.
